About Me

I received a Bachelor's of Science in Business Administration from San Francisco State University. My studies focused on strategic management and organizational design.

For me, the significance of "big data" manifested in my first role out of college at a consulting firm. We specialized in risk management and insurance solutions for big groups, and our success was driven by our ability to tell insightful stories behind the numbers. This inspired me to pursue a career in data and to learn the powerful tools that stretch beyond the realm of spreadsheets. I have a particular interest in time series analysis and natural language processing.
